Pollution Awareness


Learn about the causes and effects of pollution and ways to minimize your impact on air quality.


Vehicle Emissions

Emissions from vehicles contribute significantly to air pollution. Consider carpooling or public transportation.

Industrial Pollution

Factories release pollutants into the air. Support clean energy initiatives to reduce industrial emissions.

Household Activities

Even daily household activities, such as burning wood, can contribute to air pollution. Opt for eco-friendly practices.

Deforestation

Cutting down trees reduces the Earth's ability to absorb carbon dioxide, increasing pollution. Support reforestation and sustainable forestry practices.

Waste Management

Poor waste disposal practices, such as burning waste, release harmful toxins into the air. Reduce, reuse, and recycle to help mitigate pollution.

Energy Consumption

High energy consumption, particularly from non-renewable sources, leads to more pollution. Switch to renewable energy when possible.
